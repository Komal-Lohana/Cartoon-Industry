Who are we?

Walter Bros. Animation Industry (commonly known as Walter Bros. and abbreviated as WB), is an American diversified multinational mass media and entertainment conglomerate headquartered at the Walter Bros. Studios complex in Burbank, California, and a division of AT&T's. Founded in 1923 by Walters Brothers the company established itself as a leader in the American film industry before diversifying into animation, television, and video games, and is one of the "Big Five" major American film studios, as well as a member of the Motion Picture Association (MPA).
